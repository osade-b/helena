Welcome to COREN

The Council for the Regulation of Engineering in Nigeria (COREN) is a statutory body set up by the
Federal Government of Nigeria with the mandate to regulate the practice of Engineering in all
aspects and ramifications. It was established by Decree 55 of 1970, amended by Decree 27 of
1992 and now, Engineers (Registration, etc.) Act CAP E 11, 2004 which was further amended by the
Engineers (Registration, etc.) (Amendment)Act NO 3, 2018.
